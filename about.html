Hi, I'm Yuri.

Level Designer · Montréal, QC

I'm a level designer with a background in boardgame design, creative writing, and music. I strive to create experiences that are meaningful, memorable, and fun.

My focus is on storytelling through space — giving players the freedom to make impactful choices and feel truly part of the world. Every level is a chance to shape emotion, curiosity, and discovery.

I hold an AEC in Level Design from Campus ADN in Montréal, and have additional experience in game design, including AI and combat systems work in a professional studio context.

Outside of level design, I'm an avid tabletop RPG player, a fan of everything Larian puts out, and deeply invested in medieval fiction — all of which feed directly into how I think about space, narrative, and player experience.
